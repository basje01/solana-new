# Investor & Audience Guide

Tailoring your pitch to different audiences.

## Hackathon Judges

**What they care about**: Innovation, technical execution, completeness, potential impact
**Time**: 3-5 minutes
**Focus**: Demo > slides. Show something working.
**Tone**: Enthusiastic, technical depth welcome
**Key slides**: Problem, Solution, Demo, Why Crypto
**Skip**: Detailed financials, long market analysis
**Tip**: Judges see 50+ projects. Your first sentence must hook.

## Venture Capital (Pre-seed / Seed)

**What they care about**: Team, market size, traction, defensibility
**Time**: 20-30 minutes (with Q&A)
**Focus**: Market opportunity and your ability to capture it
**Tone**: Confident but honest about risks
**Key slides**: All 12, heavy on Market, Traction, Team
**Skip**: Nothing, but keep technical details high-level
**Tip**: VCs invest in people. Your "why you" story matters most.

## Ecosystem Grants (Solana Foundation, Superteam)

**What they care about**: Ecosystem value, open source contribution, community impact
**Time**: Written application (1-2 pages) + optional call
**Focus**: How does this benefit the Solana ecosystem?
**Tone**: Builder-focused, community-oriented
**Key slides**: Problem, Solution, Why Solana, Traction, Ask
**Skip**: Heavy financials, token economics
**Tip**: Show how other Solana builders will benefit from your work.

## Accelerators (Colosseum, Alliance)

**What they care about**: Speed of execution, coachability, market timing
**Time**: 10-15 minute interview
**Focus**: Can you ship fast? Are you listening to users?
**Tone**: Scrappy, fast-moving, open to feedback
**Key slides**: Problem, Solution, Traction, Team, Ask
**Skip**: Grand vision — focus on next 3 months
**Tip**: Show what you built in the last 2 weeks, not what you plan for next year.

## Strategic Partners (Protocols, DAOs)

**What they care about**: Integration value, mutual benefit, technical compatibility
**Time**: 30-minute call
**Focus**: How does this help THEIR users?
**Tone**: Collaborative, partnership-oriented
**Key slides**: Problem, Solution, Demo, Integration plan
**Skip**: Your fundraising plans
**Tip**: Frame everything as "here's what we do for your users."
